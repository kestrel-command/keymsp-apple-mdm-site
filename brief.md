# KeyMSP Apple Business Manager + MDM Landing Page Brief

## Goal
Create a standalone high-conversion page for KeyMSP that positions Apple Business Manager + Mobile Device Management as a practical SMB service. The page should make it clear that KeyMSP can set up and manage iPhones, iPads, and Macs with business-grade control, security, and repeatable provisioning.

## Audience
- Small and midsize businesses with 5-150 Apple devices
- Teams with remote, hybrid, or multi-location staff
- Companies that currently provision devices manually or buy devices ad hoc
- SMB owners/operations leaders who want control without hiring in-house Apple admins

## Commercial Angle
The offer should support healthy managed-service margin by emphasizing recurring value, not one-time setup only.

Primary revenue ideas:
1. Apple onboarding / enrollment project fee
2. Ongoing per-device or per-user monthly management
3. Add-on compliance, app deployment, support, replacement coordination, offboarding, and security controls

## Core Message
Apple devices can be simple for employees without becoming chaotic for the business. With Apple Business Manager and MDM, devices can arrive ready to work, stay compliant, and remain company-controlled.

## Differentiators to communicate
- Zero-touch or near-zero-touch deployment
- Central control over apps, settings, restrictions, and security baselines
- Faster onboarding and cleaner offboarding
- Better protection for company data on employee-used devices
- Local MSP partner that can both implement and operate the system

## Page sections
1. Hero
2. Problem statement
3. What Apple Business Manager + MDM actually does
4. What KeyMSP manages
5. Device lifecycle examples (new hire, lost device, terminated employee, standard app rollout)
6. Who it is for
7. Suggested packaging / service options
8. FAQ
9. CTA

## Tone
- Professional, practical, SMB-friendly
- Avoid Apple-jargon overload
- Confident but not hypey
- Sales-capable and margin-aware

## Image direction
- Clean, premium, Apple-adjacent but not using Apple trademarks improperly
- Business users with MacBook, iPhone, iPad in realistic office or remote-work settings
- Admin dashboard / fleet management visual language
- Minimal, modern, trustworthy
